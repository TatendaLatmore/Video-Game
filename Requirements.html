Algonquin College

You can go to Algonquin College you take a three year course in game development and recieve a deploma. This program provides you with hands-on learning and a team-oriented environment that simulates the work setting found in large game studios. Throughout the program, you learn the design and critical thinking skills needed to find employment in the game development industry. Requirements include English, Grade 12 (ENG4C or equivalent) Mathematics, Grade 12 (MAP4C or equivalent) Mathematics, Grade 12 (MCT4C is highly recommended) A senior credit in physics is recommended

Carlton University

At Carelton University you can take a four year course in computer science, which to get in requires six 4U or 4M credits including English (ENG4U), Advanced Functions (MHF4U), and one of Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U), Physics (SPH4U) or Computer Science (ICS4U). After your four year course yoou can do an undergraduate in computer game development. In this course in the Bachelor of Computer Science, you will learn about both the principles and practice of designing and developing modern computer games.
