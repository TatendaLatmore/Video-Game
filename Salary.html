Entry-Level Positions (Junior Game Designer)

In North America, entry-level salaries for junior game designers typically range from $40,000 to $60,000 per year.

Mid-Level Positions (Game Designer)

Mid-level game designers with a few years of experience can expect salaries ranging from $60,000 to $100,000 per year.

Senior-Level Positions (Lead Game Designer or Creative Director)

Senior game designers or those in leadership positions can earn salaries ranging from $80,000 to $150,000 or more per year, depending on their level of responsibility and the success of the projects they oversee.
